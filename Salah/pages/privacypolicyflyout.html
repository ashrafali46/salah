Privacy Policy

Personal Information Used

This app uses and may transmit your location over the internet.

How Your Location is Used

If you use automatic location or provide a city name as your location, Salah discloses this information anonymously to a geocoding web service to provide a better app experience.1, 2 If you do not want your location sent to the web service, you can opt-out by disabling automatic location and entering a latitude, longitude coordinate pair (e.g. 31.57, 74.32) as your location in Salah’s options.

When your location has been determined (via automatic location or manual entry) it is stored on your computer in the app's settings.

1 The geocoding service is provided courtesy of MapQuest, please see their Privacy Policy to learn how they use your location.

2 Geocoding converts city names (e.g. Lahore, Pakistan) to geographic locations (e.g. 31.5758° N, 74.3269° E) and vice versa, enabling easier location entry and identification.
