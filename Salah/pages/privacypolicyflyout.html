Privacy Policy

Information Used

Salah uses your location. This is a geographic location obtained from your device or provided by you.

How Your Location is Used

When you use automatic location or provide a city name as your location, Salah sends this information anonymously to a geocoding web service to provide a better application experience. Geocoding converts city names (e.g. Lahore, Pakistan) to geographic locations (e.g. 31.5758° N, 74.3269° E) and vice versa, enabling easier location entry and identification. The geocoding service is provided courtesy of MapQuest, please view their Privacy Policy to learn how they use your location. If you do not want your location sent to the web service, disable automatic location and enter a latitude, longitude coordinate pair (e.g. 31.57, 74.32) as your location in Salah’s settings.
